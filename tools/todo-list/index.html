What is Todo List?

Todo List is a free online task management tool designed with an Apple-like UI to help you organize your tasks efficiently. It allows you to create, edit, and track your tasks all in one place, with no installation required.

With its clean and intuitive interface, Todo List makes it easy to manage your daily tasks, set priorities, add due dates, and mark tasks as completed. Your tasks are automatically saved in your browser, so you can access them anytime you return to the tool.

Whether you're managing work projects, personal errands, or study assignments, Todo List helps you stay organized and focused on what matters most. The tool is designed to be simple yet powerful, providing just the right features to manage your tasks without overwhelming you with complexity.

How to Use Todo List

1

Add a New Task

Click the "+" button in the top right corner to open the Add Task modal. Enter a title for your task, and optionally add a description, due date, and priority level. Click "Add Task" to create your new task.

2

Manage Your Tasks

Click the checkbox next to a task to mark it as completed. Use the filter buttons (All, Active, Completed) to view different sets of tasks. Click on any task to view its details or edit it.

3

Edit or Delete Tasks

Click on a task to open the Edit Task modal. Here you can modify any details of the task, or click the "Delete" button to remove it completely. Your changes are saved automatically.
